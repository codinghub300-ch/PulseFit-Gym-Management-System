# PulseFit-Gym-Management-System

A modern fitness management platform designed to help gym owners, trainers, and members streamline daily operations through intelligent analytics, member management, workout tracking, and AI-powered fitness assistance.

Live Demo: https://gym-management-system-be8janwke35juesqmq3d8c.streamlit.app/

---

## Key Features
1) Owner & Administration
- Business performance dashboards and KPIs
- Revenue and membership analytics
- Trainer and staff management
- Attendance monitoring
- Reporting and export tools
- User approval and access management

2) Trainer Portal
- Member assignment management
- Workout plan creation and tracking
- Progress monitoring
- Achievement management
- Performance insights

3) Member Experience
- Personalized workout plans
- Fitness progress tracking
- Achievement monitoring
- Trainer communication
- AI-powered fitness assistance

4) AI Capabilities
- AI Fitness Coach
- Personalized workout recommendations
- Nutrition guidance
- Fitness goal planning
- Smart coaching assistance
- Gym operations support
